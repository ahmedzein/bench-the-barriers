Changing the Face of Mental Health & Addiction in Sports

Our Vision:

A knowledgeable and supportive community that provides continuous care to healthy, resilient and successful athletes.

Our Mission:

To provide education and awareness to the community about mental illness and addiction in athletes.

We Achieve Our Mission By

Offering financial relief to athletes without insurance, providing quick and easy access with limited wait times for health services and connecting athletes to specialized injury management and mental health experts.
